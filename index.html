About us

Final Fantasy XIV, Free Company

Founded by a certain bored penguin that wanted to fiddle with the free company workshop, we are a casual FC based in Tonberry who are just trying to enjoy our time in the vast land of Eorzea, be it role-playing or raiding! Our main timezone is GMT +8 but we welcome players from anywhere.

We hold activities like treasure map hunts, mount farming, learning old raid contents and holiday events.

You can visit us at Ward 16, Plot 39 in Mist Subdivision!
